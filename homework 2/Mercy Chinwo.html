ABOUT GOSPEL SINGER MERCY CHINWO


CLICK TO SEE ABOUT MERCY CHINWO Mercy Chinwo full name is Mercy Nnenda Chinwo, and she was born on 5th September 1990. She is the first child of six children. Currently , Mercy Chinwo is 31 years old. Mercy Chinwo is a Nigerian gospel musician, actress, and songwriter, who hails from Port Harcourt, River State.
Most successful gospel artist are fond of having an early start in music because they are talented, and Mercy Chinwo is a typical example. She started her musical career early in her church children choir and later migrated to the adult choir. Her mother brought up Mercy Chinwo and her siblings after losing their father during the early stages of their life.
Mercy Chinwo was into acting before she swerved full time into music. She got into the limelight in 2012 after winning season 2 of a famous acting talent show(Nigeria idol). After which in 2013, she landed her first film role in Yvonne Nelsons film, "House of Gold," featuring other stars such Yvonne Nelson, Majid Michel, and Omawumi.
In her music career, Mercy Chinwo started her musical career by voicing to musical projects by top Nigerian gospel music artists such as Sammie Okposo,Jos Praize,Buchi,Chris Morgan, and Preye, whom she featured in a single "Eze."
In 2017 Mercy Chinwo's big move came after EeZee concepts, a gospel music label owned by Mr.Ezekiel ThankGod, endorsed her. EeZee concepts has also recently signed another sprit-filled Gospel Artist-GUC[Gift Ugochi Christopher].
Mercy Chinwo released her first single, "Testimony," in 2015, and another single, "Igwe," in 2016. Her first album was in 2018, "The cross my Gaze." "The cross my Gaze" consists of many soul-uplifting gospel songs that are still trending in the Nigerian Christian space. Mercy Chinwo has collaborated with international artists. She did a remix of her most famous song, "Excess love," with JJ Hairston, Youthful Praise, and The Gratitude.
